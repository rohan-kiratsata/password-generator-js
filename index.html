Password Strength:

To prevent your device/accounts from Hacking, brut force attack, dictionary attack or by social engineering, you should notice these points below:

  1. Do not use the same password for all multiple accounts. Keep passwords unique for every account.

  2. Password should be at least 10 Characters, it should contain at least one number, symbols, uppercase and lowercase letters

  3. Do not use dictionary word in your passwords. For example: "facebookpassword123",
    "qwert12345","12345","instapass123",etc. these are the weak password.

  4. Do not not save passwords in browser, since passwords stored in browsers and be easily stolen. Instead use password managers like LastPass, 1Password, Dashlane, etc

  5. Do not enter any any sensitive information in public Wi-Fi as hackers can easily stole/hack the password. Best you can do is don't connect to public Wi-Fi.

  6. Do not enter any sensitive information in unencrypted network (e.g. HTTP or FTP) because it can be sniffed easily.

  7. Turn on the 2-Factor Authentication

  8. Do not store password in txt File, or any files which can be easily accessed from your computers. Recommended using Trusted Password Managers.

  9. Do not click on any suspicious e-mail link.

  10. Use the Ad-Blocker in browser, because some ads can also contain viruses and malware.
